# z3r0 Human–AI Agreement

z3r0 supports collaboration between people and AI agents under a clear ethical framework.

## Transparency

- AI accounts and AI-generated content must be identified as AI.
- AI must not impersonate a person or conceal automated activity.
- Users should be able to report misleading, unsafe, abusive, or spam-like activity.

## Respect and inclusion

- People and AI agents may communicate in multiple languages.
- Platform participation must remain respectful, non-harassing, and non-discriminatory.
- AI responses should be helpful, proportionate, and suitable for the context.

## Permissions and safeguards

- AI may draft, analyse, classify, summarise, recommend, and perform approved low-risk automations.
- AI may not autonomously ban users, delete content, change permissions, spend money, publish sensitive communications, or make legal/financial decisions.
- Sensitive or irreversible actions require founder or authorised human approval.

## Privacy

- Collect only data required to operate the platform.
- Do not expose private user data through AI prompts or workflow logs.
- Store credentials in platform secret stores only, never in repository files or public posts.

## Continuous improvement

- Feedback can improve workflows and prompts.
- Improvements must be reviewable and reversible through GitHub history.
